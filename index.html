About Me

I'm thrilled to share my journey in the world of music production, which spans over four incredible years. Hailing from the vibrant city of Warakapola, I have immersed myself in the captivating realm of electronic music.

As a passionate electronic music producer, DJ, and aspiring undergraduate audio engineer, I have honed my skills to create mesmerizing soundscapes that transport listeners on unforgettable sonic adventures. Join me as I continue to explore the boundless possibilities of music and express my creative vision through the power of sound.
